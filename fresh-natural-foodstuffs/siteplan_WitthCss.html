1. My Site Name

Fresh & Natural Foodstuffs

The name "Fresh & Natural Foodstuffs" was chosen to clearly communicate the core values of the business:

  • Fresh: Emphasizes the quality and freshness of products
  • Natural: Highlights the organic and unprocessed nature of offerings
  • Foodstuffs: Clearly indicates this is a food-related business with diverse product range

This name positions the business as a trusted source for quality, natural food products in the local Nigerian market, particularly appealing to health-conscious consumers and those seeking authentic local ingredients.

2. Site Purpose

The Fresh & Natural Foodstuffs website serves as a comprehensive digital marketplace and information hub with the following primary purposes:

Primary Objectives:

  • Online Product Catalog: Showcase the complete range of fresh and natural food products available
  • E-commerce Platform: Enable customers to browse, select, and initiate purchases through WhatsApp integration
  • Business Information: Provide details about the company's mission, values, and commitment to quality
  • Customer Connection: Facilitate communication between customers and the business

Specific Functions:

  • Display products with images, descriptions, and prices
  • Allow product categorization and search functionality
  • Enable shopping cart functionality for order compilation
  • Provide WhatsApp integration for order confirmation and pricing discussion
  • Offer company information and contact details
  • Ensure mobile-responsive design for accessibility

3. Scenarios

These scenarios represent typical user journeys and help define the target audience and their needs:

Scenario 1: The Busy Working Professional

User: Chinedu, a 32-year-old accountant living in Benin City

Situation: Chinedu works long hours and has limited time for grocery shopping. He wants to prepare traditional Nigerian meals but struggles to find quality ingredients during his limited free time.

Goals:

  • Find fresh, authentic Nigerian food ingredients online
  • Compare prices and product quality from his office or home
  • Place orders conveniently and arrange for delivery
  • Get assistance with product selection and pricing

How the website helps: The website provides a comprehensive product catalog with clear images and descriptions, allowing Chinedu to browse during breaks. The WhatsApp integration lets him quickly discuss prices and arrange delivery without visiting physical markets.

Scenario 2: The Diaspora Customer

User: Amara, a 40-year-old Nigerian living in the United Kingdom

Situation: Amara wants to send authentic Nigerian food items to her elderly parents in Benin City. She's unfamiliar with current local prices and needs reassurance about product quality.

Goals:

  • Find authentic Nigerian foodstuffs for her parents
  • Ensure product quality and freshness
  • Arrange delivery to her parents' home
  • Make payments from abroad

How the website helps: The website provides detailed product information and high-quality images that give Amara confidence in her selections. The WhatsApp business integration allows her to communicate directly with the seller to discuss specific needs, confirm prices, and arrange international payment and local delivery.

Scenario 3: The Local Restaurant Owner

User: Chef Bola, owner of a local restaurant specializing in traditional cuisine

Situation: Chef Bola needs regular, reliable supplies of fresh ingredients for her restaurant. She wants to establish a relationship with a trusted supplier who can provide consistent quality.

Goals:

  • Find a reliable supplier of fresh, natural ingredients
  • Compare bulk pricing options
  • Establish a ongoing business relationship
  • Arrange regular deliveries

How the website helps: The website showcases the range of available products with clear pricing. The business contact information and WhatsApp integration allow Chef Bola to discuss bulk orders, negotiate pricing, and set up a regular supply arrangement directly with the business owners.

4. Color Schema

The color palette was carefully selected to reflect the natural, fresh theme of the business while ensuring good readability and visual appeal.

Primary Green

#4CAF50

Usage: Primary buttons, headers, links, and accents

Accent Yellow

#FFC107

Usage: Secondary actions, highlights, and special notices

Alert Orange

#FF5722

Usage: Discount badges, important alerts, and call-to-action elements

Color Application:

Element Color Purpose
Primary Buttons Primary Green (#4CAF50) Main call-to-action elements like "Add to Cart"
Secondary Buttons Accent Yellow (#FFC107) Secondary actions and alternative buttons
Discount Badges Alert Orange (#FF5722) Highlight special offers and discounts
Header & Navigation Primary Green (#4CAF50) Brand identification and main navigation
Text & Background Black/White & Grays Ensure readability and clean design

5. Typography

The typography system uses Google Fonts to ensure consistency and readability across all devices.

Poppins - Headings

Poppins is used for all headings and important text elements. Its modern, clean appearance conveys professionalism while maintaining approachability.

Usage: H1, H2, H3 headings, navigation items, buttons

Roboto - Body Text

Roboto is used for body text and paragraph content. Its excellent readability and neutral character make it ideal for longer text passages.

Usage: Paragraphs, product descriptions, form labels, footer text

Font Hierarchy:

Element Font Family Size/Weight Usage Example
H1 - Main Headings Poppins 2.5rem, 700 Page titles, main banners
H2 - Section Headings Poppins 2rem, 600 Section titles
H3 - Subheadings Poppins 1.5rem, 500 Content group headings
Body Text Roboto 1rem, 400 Paragraphs, descriptions
Buttons & Navigation Poppins 1rem, 500 Interactive elements
